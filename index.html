About Me


My name is Evan Cleary. I was born in a suburb outside of Philadelphia called Ambler, PA. I am one of 6 siblings in my family. I have 2 brothers and 3 sisters. All of them are older then me, including my twin. My twin is a Fraternal twin and is a girl named Rory. She lives in Boston now.

I went to Villanova University where I was president of the service organization I was a part of. I then went on to help fundraise for a Bio-Tech start-up based around orthotics for people with drop-foot syndrome. I later went on to start my own start-up which is a Property & Casualty insurance agency that specializes in working with clients during the Financial Planning process.

Growing up I've had many ideas for cool utilizations of technology. However to this day they remain just ideas that I can't formulate on my own. Instead of relying on the work of others to carry out my vision, I have decided to learn the skills myself. Therefore I can carry my visions out utilizing self-motivation. I hope to make many different webapplications and mobile applications with my skillset that can help make the world a better place. I have one idea in particular that I believe can be world-changing.
